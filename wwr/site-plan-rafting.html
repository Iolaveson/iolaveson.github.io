Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share the joy and thrill of white water rafting. This site will accomplish this by sharing engaging and easy to understand information about this fun activity. We will also offer guided white water rafting experiences while still offering materials for the more experienced rafting enthuiast.

Audience

The audience for this is primarily those who do not like or haven't yet experienced white water rafting. The secondary audience is those who are rafting enthuiasts. The needs we will address is the fears of participating in this activity. This site will have standard support for desktop/laptop.
